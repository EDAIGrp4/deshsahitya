FAQs Panel

How do I borrow a book?

You can borrow a book by visiting the library and presenting your library card at the circulation desk. The librarian will assist you in the process.

How many books can I borrow at a time?

You can borrow up to 5 books at a time. However, this limit may vary depending on your membership type and library policies.

How long can I keep a borrowed book?

The standard loan period for books is 3 weeks. You can renew a book if no one else has reserved it, for an additional 3 weeks.
